Abstract  
With the transnational flow of goods, people and capital, the global economy becomes  
more and more interdependent. From the traditional international trade to the development of  
emerging trade, service industry has gradually become a hot industry in international trade.  
However, due to trade barriers, trade frictions and other reasons, countries are increasingly  
inclined to make foreign direct investment in services. Since the reform and opening up in  
1978, the actual use of foreign investment in China's service industry has been increasing year  
by year, and it has broken through the $100 billion mark in 2010. In 2013, China has become  
the second largest economy in the world and the second largest country in attracting foreign  
investment. In 2019, FDI in service industry has reached nearly $90 billion. It can be seen that  
China's degree of opening to the outside world is higher and higher, and its influence in the  
global economy is increasingly significant. However, although the scale of China's service  
trade continues to expand, it still faces many problems, such as the low level of service export  
technology, trade deficit and so on. Only by continuously improving the structure of service  
export and increasing the technical complexity of service export, can the status quo be  
reversed and China's International competitiveness be improved.  
With the continuous expansion of FDI in service industry, countries are increasingly  
concerned about the improvement of service export technology. Therefore, based on the  
perspective of the host country, this paper discusses the relationship between FDI in China's  
service industry and the technical complexity of service export. The main contents include the  
following aspects: firstly, it combs the relevant literature of export technical complexity,  
including the measurement, influencing factors and economic effects of export technical  
complexity; secondly, it analyzes the development status of FDI in China's service industry  
from the perspective of service industry as a whole and departments; thirdly, it calculates and  
analyzes the technical complexity of China's service export; fourthly, it analyzes the  
development status of FDI in China's service industry from the perspective of service industry  
as a whole and departments Theoretical analysis and empirical test are used to explain the  
impact of FDI in service industry on the technical complexity of service export; finally,  
relevant countermeasures and suggestions are put forward.
